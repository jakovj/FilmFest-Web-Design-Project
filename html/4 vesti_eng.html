Tickets for the first film fest in the sale of Saturday 19th May!

The tickets for the festival will be released for sale on Saturday, May 19th. Projection cards in the Sava Center and the Youth House, at the box offices of these halls, can also be purchased online via Eventim, as well as at all Eventim outlets.
